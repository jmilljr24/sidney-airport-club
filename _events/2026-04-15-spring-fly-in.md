Join us for our annual Spring Fly-In event! This is a great opportunity to meet fellow pilots, enjoy some great food, and showcase your aircraft.

## Event Details

- **Date:** April 15, 2026
- **Time:** 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Location:** Sidney Airport Main Ramp

## Activities

- Aircraft displays
- Pilot safety briefing at noon
- BBQ lunch provided
- Raffle prizes

All pilots and aviation enthusiasts are welcome. Fuel discounts available for participating aircraft.

For more information, contact the club president.
