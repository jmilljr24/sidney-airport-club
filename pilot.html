Courtesy Car

A courtesy car is available for use at the airport, generously provided by the Village of Sidney.

The vehicle may be checked out during attended airport hours. Please call ahead for current hours of operation and vehicle availability.

Requirements:

  • A valid driver's license
  • Proof of current automobile insurance

Fuel Policy:

Please return the vehicle with a full tank of fuel.

After-Hours Returns:

If returning the vehicle after hours, park it in the designated area and place the keys in the airport drop box.

We appreciate your cooperation in helping keep this service available for all visitors.
